PRIVACY POLICY

Send Browser TV
Last updated: July 2025

------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Send Browser TV ("the App", "we", "us",
or "our") handles information when you use our Android application.

Send Browser TV is a utility app that allows users to browse the web,
download APK files on their smartphone, and send/install them on a TV or
TV Stick over a local Wi-Fi network using ADB (Android Debug Bridge)
over TCP port 5555.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what
data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

3. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

Send Browser TV does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data. Specifically:

• We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, phone number,
  address, etc.)
• We do NOT collect device identifiers (Advertising ID, IMEI, etc.)
• We do NOT collect analytics or usage statistics
• We do NOT use tracking technologies, cookies, or pixels for tracking
  purposes
• We do NOT collect browsing history — all browsing activity within the
  built-in browser stays on your device
• We do NOT use third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics,
  Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, etc.)
• We do NOT serve advertisements
• We do NOT sell, rent, or share any data with third parties

------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. DATA THAT STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE

The following data is generated and used locally on your device only and
is never transmitted to us or any third party:

a) Downloaded files (APKs)
   APK files you download through the built-in browser are saved locally
   on your device (in the app's internal folder or a user-selected
   folder via Android Storage Access Framework). These files are under
   your full control.

b) IP addresses of TVs / TV Sticks
   When you use the Manual IP or Auto Scan feature, IP addresses of
   devices on your local network are used solely to establish an ADB
   connection. These addresses are not stored persistently, logged, or
   transmitted outside your local network.

c) ADB connection data
   The App communicates with your TV / TV Stick over your local Wi-Fi
   network using the ADB protocol (TCP 5555). This communication occurs
   exclusively within your local network and does not pass through any
   external server.

d) Browser cache and cookies
   The built-in browser (GeckoView) may store temporary cache and
   cookies locally on your device, as any standard browser does. This
   data is never accessed or transmitted by us.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. PERMISSIONS

The App may request the following Android permissions:

• INTERNET
  Required to browse the web in the built-in browser and to communicate
  with your TV / TV Stick over the local Wi-Fi network.

• ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_WIFI_STATE
  Required to detect network connectivity and identify the local subnet
  for the Auto Scan feature.

• WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (if applicable)
  Required on older Android versions to save downloaded APK files to
  device storage.

• MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (if applicable)
  May be required on Android 11+ to access files in shared storage.

• FOREGROUND_SERVICE (if applicable)
  May be used to keep downloads or ADB transfers running reliably in
  the background.

No permission is used to collect or transmit personal data.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Send Browser TV is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the
applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any
personal information from children. Since the App does not collect any
data at all, no data from children is collected.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. DATA SECURITY

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there
is no risk of a data breach from our side.

All operations (browsing, downloading, ADB connections) are performed
locally on your device and within your local Wi-Fi network.

Please note that the ADB connection between your phone and your TV /
TV Stick is not encrypted. We recommend using the App only on trusted
private Wi-Fi networks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. DATA RETENTION

We do not retain any data. All data generated by the App (downloads,
cache, connection information) is stored locally on your device and can
be deleted at any time by:

• Clearing the App's data/cache in Android Settings
• Uninstalling the App
• Manually deleting downloaded files

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for
us to provide, modify, or delete. However, you have full control over
all data stored locally on your device.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have
rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are a
California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer
Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we collect no personal data, these rights are
inherently respected.
